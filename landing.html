Autonomous Line Follower Robot

This robot used an array of light sensors to follow a black line painted on a white surface. The project was for a design competition within a mechanical engineering course. After hundreds of hours of prototyping and iterations, our robot was a semifinalist in the competition. If you are curious in reading the report our group curated which details all of the 3D Modeling, Cost Analysis, BOM, etc., you may view it here: Report

Reverse Engineering Project

This project's objective was to reverse engineer a staple gun to determine how it works and how it compares to similar products on the market. Every single part was 3D modeled and itemized in a BOM. If you would like to see the report you can click here: Report

Handmade Clock

This clock was made by me by following GD&T drawings and using subtractive manufacturing techniques in the MECH shop. The base was created on a vertical milling machine and then a CNC machine for the engraving, the pen holders were created on a lathe, and the face was drilled on a drill press.
